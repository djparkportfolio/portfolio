While my time at SL Corporation, I participated in developing in-house activity call “Open Faucet”. Open faucet (OF) is SL’s own strategic product development process. Through OF, marketing team gathers the interested parties into a task force team for upcoming advanced product development for successful project management. I developed OF’s identity and branding for friendly approach for internal PR to help OF efficiently settle down in the company.
